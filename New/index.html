My name is Khoa, and I'm a part-time freelance social media content creator, photographer, marketer and science student. After graduating with a Bachelor of Biomedicine (Immunology), I'm currently finishing a Master of Biotechnology at the University of Melbourne while actively pursuing opportunities in marketing, communications and photography.

Sitting at the intersection between creative visual storytelling and a burning passion for science, I found myself thriving at analytics-driven marketing, content optimisation and bridging the gap between complex topics and effective communication.

My biggest dream is to facilitate science adaptation and education through well thought-out messaging, visually engaging content and memorable ideas.
